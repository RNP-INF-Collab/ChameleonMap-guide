Welcome to ChameleonMap guide!

April 28, 2022 - ChameleonMap v1.2 has been released!

ChamaleonMap is the leading open-source system for creation of interactive maps with geolocated database and was developed in partneship between the Infomatic Institute of Federal University of Rio Grande do Sul (INF-UFRGS) and the Brazilian National Research Network (RNP).The system is incredible flexible and adaptative like a Chamaleon, allowing its use in many diferent domains with many diferent contexts, since computer networks until economics. In addition the system is also user-friendly for both the system administrator and the final user.

This guide is a compilation of the main topics of the system's use and managing. Select a topic in the left sidebar to get more information of it.
